## 2026-05-13 - VoiceOver verbosity on decorative chevrons\n**Learning:** VoiceOver reads decorative SF Symbols (like chevrons and toggle indicators) by default if they are inside accessible elements, adding unnecessary noise when the label already explains the action.\n**Action:** Add `.accessibilityHidden(true)` to purely visual or decorative images, particularly those inside buttons or navigation links that already have an explicit `.accessibilityLabel` or descriptive `Text`.
